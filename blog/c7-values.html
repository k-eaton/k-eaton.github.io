Sterotype Threat part 2

  • January 18th, 2015
  • cultural, sterotype threat, values are what you assign to variables
  • Accomplishment
  • Achievement
  • Close Relationships
  • Creativity
  • Humor

If I had to pick from the list, I'd say Humor and Creativity are definitely values I try to live by. The others are nice, but are more like goals to attain. Making creative, funny things is kind of my personal mission statement. It's definitely when I am happiest.

The last thing someone asked my advice on was about some JavaScript. And it did relate to my values: I find coding highly creative. And being able to help gave me a sense of accomplishment.

Can my values help mediate sterotype threat? Possibly. I may be a girl, but I'm good at problem solving, which is creative. Mostly though, I just have to remember that sterotype threat exists, and apply it to any given experience.
